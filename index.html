A Little About Me!

Hello! I want to thank you for stopping by to check out my portfolio and learning a little about me! I'm going to keep this professional by detailing my skills and experience to offer you a broader view of my capabilities beyond what I can do inside a resume.

I am a mostly self-taught developer who first step foot into the world of tech and programming when I volunteered to build the family business a website to help boost our digital foothold. I utilized everything I could find at my disposal from online bootcamps like freecodecamp and Codecademy to YouTube tutorials and the StackOverflow forums. This led me to re-enroll in college and pursue a second Bachelors degree in the computer science field which I achieved in August, 2023 when I received my degree in Digital Media and Web Technology from the University of Maryland Global Campus.

My coursework at UMGC provided me many opportunities to learn a variety of skills pertaining to web development, software engineering and graphic design. A few of these skills include:

  • HTML5 & CSS programming languages
  • JavaScript (including the jQuery, Ajax and JSON libraries)
  • LESS (CSS preprocessor)
  • UX and UI principles
  • Adobe Creative Suite (Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD)

After a few years of self-teaching and a year of collegiate level coursework I discovered I had a real passion for programming as well as researching and solving problems. I continued my own self-taught education by expanding on what I learned in college to more advanced forms of the programming languages as well as industry standard tools and programming languages more suited to application development. In addition to the programming languages I learned in school I also have sufficient knowledge in the following:

  • React.js JavaScript library

I am continuing to learn even more! Currently I am learning Python as well as experimenting using Java to build an application that works on the Android OS.
